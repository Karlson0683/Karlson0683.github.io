About Me



While graduating University I also worked in USA, where I learned importance of excelent customer service on several sales representative positions. After graduation I became self-employed and established my own brand called Garant baits / sport fishing brand, fishing just for fun not meat. In several years I had built this company into one of the most popular fishing brand in Slovakia, which by that time reached a number of successful milestones. In between I learned a lot about marketing, graphic design, network and team building, international business ect.. I learned brand building mindset. For more information please download my CV .

Finally in 2017 the company was sold.

After that I felt a strong need to change the sport fishing environment and started to travel and work abroad. I worked on different positions in logistics, and also as business sales representative. Lived in countries such as Norway, Austria, France and spent 6 months in India with enlightenment master Sadhguru, where in his Isha Yoga Ashram I learned several different yoga practices from the original source.

In 2019 I started to intensively learn how to code by paid courses from Ing. Anton Šutiak with focus on html, css, Js, php, mySql, what I practice until today. Already built several project, see more in " My projects " section.

From 2020 until today I work part timely as sport data analysis for World wide sport company and reached world wide acreditation  for Footbal, Ice Hockey and Handball.
